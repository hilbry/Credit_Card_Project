To raise awareness and educate consumers about credit card fraud and how they can protect themselves.

Definition of credit card fraud

According to a Cornell Law School page,
https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/credit_card_fraud, “Credit card fraud is a form of identity theft that involves an unauthorized taking of another’s credit card information for the purpose of charging purchases to the account or removing funds from it.”
